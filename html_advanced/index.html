Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best makeup products for oily skin?

For oily skin, it's recommended to use oil-free foundations, mattifying primers, and setting powders to control shine and increase the longevity of makeup.

How do I choose the right foundation shade?

To find the right foundation shade, it's best to test the shade on your jawline or wrist to match it with your skin tone. Consider your undertone (cool, warm, or neutral) to ensure a perfect match.

What is the purpose of a primer?

A primer creates a smooth base for makeup application, helps to blur imperfections, extends the wear of makeup, and enhances the overall finish of the makeup look.

How do I make my lipstick last longer?

To make your lipstick last longer, start by exfoliating your lips, apply a lip primer or concealer as a base, line and fill in your lips with a lip liner, apply the lipstick, and set it with a thin layer of translucent powder.
